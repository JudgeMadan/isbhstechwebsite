About Us

     As our goal is to help maximize learning efficiency through technology in the ISB High School community, you can find solutions to your common technology issues AND learn cool computer tips that will maximize your computer-using abilities like never before. Simply click on one of the subpages (on the navigation bar to the top) to select where you want to go! To approach us, simply find an HS Tech member in YOUR classroom (identified by a sticker on their computer), or approach our Tech Bar right outside the cafeteria (Mr. Hochberg's old room) during your free period! In addition, our club has regular meetings every Wednesday, right after school (during flex time) at the Multipurpose Building (MPB), so pop by if you need any live assistance or help!
